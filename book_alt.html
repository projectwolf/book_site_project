The Fate of Man Lies Not in Their Hands...

Wars, genetic mutations, and unexplained disappearances have brought the human population down to roughly three billion worldwide. The drug, Alterare, was supposed to cure diseases and slow down aging amongst other benefits, but instead it did the exact opposite and killed many. Years passed after the chaos caused by Alterare and people settled in new cities and locations, while others restored former homes. A special organization was formed to track down and stop those who desire to ressurect Alterare or start wars. Hagen is one of the elite soldiers in the special organization that has inherited unique genetic abilities which was a different side-effect of Alterare. Caleianna is the genius behind the new infrastructure of cyber-security systems and technology that protects the Alterare and other valuable data from falling into the wrong hands. Together along with their trusted allies, the two will fight at all cost to defend their city and loved ones from being ruined by Zurrias, the leader of a well-known terrorist group, who will stop at nothing from accomplishing his diabolical plans.

Will truth and love set lives free from selfish desires that lurk in the human heart or will darkness lead the way to another war?
